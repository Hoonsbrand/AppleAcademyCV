
# RPG

## The goal of this game
Keep player's HP and get rid of all of the monsters!

## How to play
- Move : W, A, S, D, ←, →, ↓, ↑, JoyStick Controller
- Attack : Mouse right click
- Zoom in and out : Mouse scroll
