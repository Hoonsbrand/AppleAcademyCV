**Interface**

* Red block - Player
* Orange block - Enemy

**How to play** 

* Player move with left and right key of Arrow key.
* The game is over When the player hit by the enemy.
* The player is win when the player do not hit by enemy at all within the total time. ( 30 seconds.)
